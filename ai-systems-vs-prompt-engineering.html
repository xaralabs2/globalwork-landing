AI Systems vs Prompt Engineering

Prompt engineering focuses on inputs. AI systems engineering focuses on control, reliability, and accountability.

Why Prompt Engineering Breaks in Production

Prompts do not provide governance, audit trails, escalation logic, or cost control.

What AI Systems Engineering Includes

  • Multi-step workflows
  • Human-in-the-loop oversight
  • Agent permissions
  • Logging and auditability

Career Impact

Prompt roles are fragile. AI systems roles are long-term, trusted, and scarce.
