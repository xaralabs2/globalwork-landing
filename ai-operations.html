What Is AI Operations?

AI Operations (AI Ops) is the discipline of designing, supervising, and controlling AI systems once they are deployed into real production environments.

Why AI Operations Exists

Most AI failures do not come from bad models. They come from uncontrolled automation, missing oversight, cost explosions, and lack of accountability after deployment.

What AI Operations Controls

  • Which AI models are allowed to run
  • What agents are permitted to do
  • How workflows execute and escalate
  • How decisions are logged and audited

AI Operations vs MLOps

MLOps focuses on training and deploying models. AI Operations focuses on controlling behavior, reliability, and human oversight in live systems.

Careers in AI Operations

AI Operator, AI Systems Engineer, Agentic Systems Engineer, AI Platform Architect.
